Regular web development on the other hand is running quite smoothly and reliably and should remain as is. With proper processes in place the ecomm team can focus on high level priorities while the content team could help step in and fill in gaps when available – because we’re multi-talented!

To produce brand-level-quality content with the least amount of resources possible.
